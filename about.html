About our Restaurant

Brought to you by iSabelo

iSabelo

Founded in 2015 by Lunga Thwala, iSabelo is a restaurant located in Florida, Rooderport that strives to offers fresh and healty food at an offordable price.

iSabelo was founded on the idea that the food you eat can be either the safest and most powerful form of medicine.

Our History

  • Our Experience
